You're invited

to Mexican fiesta!

The details:

  • bring your own drinks
  • bring your own sombrero
  • bring your best mood for some games and interesting activities

Women

Tips for looks:

Outfit:

  • Wear skirts, pants or blouses in the flag colors, or simply accent your clothing with scarves around your waist or neck. For example, wear a red skirt and a white blouse with a bright green scarf at your waist and a white gardenia in your hair.


Hairstyle:

  • Long locks swept back or teased to sit off the face. Often pinned into a large and high quiff. Sometimes secured with a bandana.

Accessories:

  • Wind fan matching your outfit colors can be a great accessory or a great solution in a hot day.
  • Mexican maracas are also a great accsessory for the party

Men

Tips for looks:

Outfit:

  • Lightweight pants and short-sleeved shirts are the normal. If you want pop out in the crowd, you can wear some bright colors, multicolor poncho or "hombre" costume in mexican flag colors.




Hairstyle:

  • If you really want to get in character, trim the sides and keep a little bit longer hair on top. Also you can wear a bandana representing mexican flag colors or a sombrero.



Accesossories:

  • Buy some mustaches with a sticky tape on the other side, great idea is also an old shotter vest, where you can put your drinks or a fake gun.
